reivindican	claim / reclaim / defend / make up	Principal Translations
reivindicar⇒ vtr	(derecho: recuperar, retomar)	reclaim vtr
 	Reivindicaron el derecho al voto y a la libre expresión.
 	They reclaimed the right to vote and the right to free expression.
reivindicar vtr	(defender, argumentar)	defend vtr
 	Los obreros intentaron reivindicar las razones de su lucha.
 	The workers tried to defend the reasons for their struggle.
reivindicarse v prnl	(enmendarse)  (restore relations)	make up vi phrasal
 	  (regain standing)	redeem yourself, vindicate yourself v expr
 	  (informal: heal emotional damage)	mend fences vtr + n
 	Pidió perdón y se reivindicó con la familia.
 	He asked for forgiveness and made up with the family.
reivindicar vtr	(autoría: reclamar)	claim vtr
 	El grupo terrorista reivindicó su responsabilidad en el ataque a la embajada.
 	The terrorist group claimed responsibility in the embassy attack.
